Macarona béchamel recipe

Description

Macarona béchamel is the Egyptian take on a Bechamel pasta bake. It's usually made with a mix of penne pasta, a savory seasoned ground beef filling, and a rich, creamy béchamel sauce. The layers are baked until golden, creating a delicious combination of tender pasta and creamy sauce.

This hearty casserole is a favorite at family gatherings and special occasions and is often served with a fresh green salad.

Ingredients

  • 500 g penne or macaroni pasta
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella or cheddar cheese (optional)

Directions

  1. Cook the pasta until just al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a skillet and cook the onion until softened.
  3. Add the garlic and ground beef, cooking until the meat is browned.
  4. Season with salt, black pepper, and cinnamon if using.
  5. In a saucepan, melt the butter and whisk in the flour.
  6. Gradually add the milk while whisking until the sauce becomes smooth and thick.
  7. Remove the sauce from the heat and stir in the beaten egg.
  8. Spread half of the pasta in a baking dish and cover with a layer of the meat mixture.
  9. Add the remaining pasta and pour the béchamel sauce evenly over the top.
  10. Sprinkle with cheese if desired.
  11. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 40 to 45 minutes, or until the top is golden brown.
  12. Let the casserole rest for 10 minutes before serving.
