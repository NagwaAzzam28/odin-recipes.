Egyptian Kofta

Description

Egyptian kofta is a flavorful grilled meat dish made with ground beef, fresh parsley, onions, and a blend of warm Middle Eastern spices. The meat is shaped onto skewers or formed into logs before being grilled until tender and lightly charred.

Traditionally served with pita bread, rice, grilled vegetables, or fresh salad, Egyptian kofta is a popular family meal that is both simple to prepare and packed with rich, savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • 500 g (1 lb) ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely grated
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (optional)

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, grated onion, parsley, garlic, cumin, coriander, paprika, black pepper, and salt.
  2. Mix the ingredients until they are evenly combined, but avoid overmixing. Shape the mixture into long oval logs around skewers or form them by hand if skewers are not available.
  3. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat and lightly oil the surface.
  4. Grill the kofta for about 4 to 5 minutes per side, turning occasionally until browned and cooked through.
  5. Remove from the grill and let the kofta rest for a few minutes before serving.
  6. Serve warm with pita bread, tahini sauce, grilled vegetables, or a fresh salad.
